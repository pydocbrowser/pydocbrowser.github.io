def _check_for_name_in_memos(self, checkfirst, kw): (source) ¶

Look in the 'ddl runner' for 'memos', then note our name in that collection. This to ensure a particular named type is operated upon only once within any kind of create/drop sequence without relying upon "checkfirst".

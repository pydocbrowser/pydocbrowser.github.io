django.db.models.fields.reverse_related

module documentation
(source)

"Rel objects" for related fields. "Rel objects" (for lack of a better name) carry information about the relation modeled by a related field and provide some utility functions. They're stored in the ``remote_field`` attribute of the field. They also act as reverse fields for the purposes of the Meta API because they're the closest concept currently available.

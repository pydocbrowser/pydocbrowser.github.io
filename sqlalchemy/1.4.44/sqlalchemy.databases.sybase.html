.. dialect:: sybase :name: Sybase .. note:: The Sybase dialect within SQLAlchemy **is not currently supported**. It is not tested within continuous integration and is likely to have many issues and caveats not currently handled. Consider using the `external dialect <https://github.com/gordthompson/sqlalchemy-sybase>`_ instead. .. deprecated:: 1.4 The internal Sybase dialect is deprecated and will be removed in a future version. Use the external dialect.
